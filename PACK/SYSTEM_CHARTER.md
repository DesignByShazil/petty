## Core Promise
Every user should be able to help pet owners arrive at the vet with a clear, organised, trustworthy picture of what has been happening.

## Pack Type
Web SaaS

## Problem Being Solved
Pet owners often notice things in fragments:

scratching more than usual
eating less
strange stool
limping on and off
low energy
vomiting once, then seeming okay
change after a new food, treat, medicine, or environment

The problem is that this information gets lost, forgotten, or explained badly when the owner finally speaks to the vet.

So the vet receives:

incomplete timelines
vague descriptions
missing photos/videos
no symptom history
no clarity on what changed and when

That creates stress for the owner, less efficient appointments, and weaker continuity of care.

Core pain points
owners forget key details
symptom patterns are hard to track over time
multiple caretakers create confusion
vet visits are rushed and fragmented
chronic issues need consistent monitoring
aftercare and medication adherence are messy
there is no simple bridge between “what I noticed at home” and “what the vet needs to know”
