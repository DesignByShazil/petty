# System Charter — Petty

## Mission
Help pet owners arrive at the vet with a clear, organised, trustworthy picture of what has been happening.

## Core Promise
You notice something. Petty helps you remember it, pattern it, and hand it to the vet cleanly.

## Pack Type
Web SaaS

---

## Target User

**Primary:** Dog and cat owners who are actively engaged in their pet's wellbeing — especially those managing a recurring or chronic issue.

**Best first niche:** owners of dogs with recurring skin, stomach, anxiety, or recovery-related issues. They monitor constantly, visit the vet repeatedly, track symptoms informally, and feel real emotional weight. They will pay for anything that reduces stress and improves care.

**Secondary audiences:**
- Cat owners with chronic or behavioral concerns
- Owners of senior pets
- Post-surgery recovery households
- New rescue / adoption households still observing
- Clinics that want better-prepared clients (distribution channel, not direct customer)

---

## Problem

Pet owners notice things in fragments: more scratching today, ate half the bowl, one-off vomit, limp that comes and goes, low energy after a new treat. By the time they're in the vet's office, the details are blurry and the vet gets:

- Incomplete timelines
- Vague descriptions
- Missing photos and videos
- No pattern over time
- Confusion across multiple caretakers

Result: stress for the owner, rushed appointments, weaker continuity of care.

## Core Pain Points
- Owners forget key details
- Symptom patterns are hard to see over time
- Multiple caretakers create confusion
- Vet visits are rushed
- Chronic issues need consistent monitoring
- Aftercare and medication adherence are messy
- No bridge between "what I noticed at home" and "what the vet needs to know"
